parts of a home

M E N U

i created this archive as part of the final project for medp250: web production i, as part of my hunter college media studies major. confined to solely my house during the 2020 covid pandemic, i sought to break down the many parts that make up the idea of "home" and showcase said parts on this site. through this exercise, i discovered parts and angles of my house i'd never noticed or given second thought to before.
